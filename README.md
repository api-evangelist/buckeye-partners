# Buckeye Partners (buckeye-partners)
Buckeye Partners, LP is a master limited partnership that owns and operates one of the largest independent liquid petroleum product pipeline and terminal systems in the United States. Buckeye transports and stores refined petroleum products including gasoline, jet fuel, diesel, and heating oil, connecting refineries and import terminals to local distribution markets across the Eastern US, Midwest, and internationally. Buckeye was taken private by IFM Investors in 2019.
